DANIEL STEINS - OWNER

DANIELS STEINS STARTED HIS PROFESSIONAL CAREER IN THE CONSTRUCTION INDUSTRY AFTER GRADUATING FROM COLLEGE WITH AN UNDERGRADUATE DEGREE IN CIVIL ENGINEERING, HOWEVER, HIS ADMIRATION FOR THE CONSTRUCTION INDUSTRY BEGAN MUCH EARLIER IN HIS LIFE. GROWING UP AROUND CONSTRUCTION AND WITNESSING FIRSTHAND THE USE OF LARGE EQUIPMENT ON CONSTRUCTION PROJECTS AND BEING ABLE TO VISUALLY SEE THE RESULTS OF HARD WORK, SKILLED TRADES, EFFECTIVE COMMUNICATION, AND DEVELOPING AND EXECUTING A PLAN HAS CAPTIVATED HIM THROUGHOUT HIS EARLY YEARS AND STILL TO THIS DAY.

AFTER GRADUATING FROM COLLEGE, DANIEL JOINED AN INDUSTRY LEADING GENERAL CONTRACTOR WHERE HE SPENT NEARLY A DECADE BUILDING NUMEROUS LARGE SCALE COMMERCIAL CIVIL INFRASTRUCTURE PROJECTS THROUGHOUT ARIZONA AND THE UNITED STATES AS A WHOLE. IT WAS THROUGHOUT THOSE YEARS THAT DANIELS’ PASSION FOR THE CONSTRUCTION INDUSTRY WAS TRULY ENGRAINED IN HIM. DURING THAT TIME DANIEL WAS FORTUNATE TO RISE THROUGH THE RANKS AND BUILD ON HIS SKILLS TO BE A LEADER AMONGST TEAMS, KNOWLEDGE TO TAKE ON AND BUILD COMPLEXT PROJECTS SAFELY, EFFICIENTLY, AT THE LOWEST COST, HIGHEST QUALITY, AND SKILLS TO COMMUNICATE EFFECTIVLEY WITH CLIENTS, OWNERS, AND OWNER'S REPRESENTATIVES.

SINCE EARLY ON IN HIS LIFE, DANIEL HAD SET A GOAL FOR HIMSELF TO START HIS OWN CONSTRUCTION ORGANIZATION WHEN THE OPPORTUNITY AND HIS EXPERIENCE LEVEL ALIGNED. NOT TO BE ONE TO LET HIS GOALS PASS HIM BY AND IN COMBINATION WITH HIS STRIVE FOR RESPONSIBILITY, DRIVE TO SUCCEED, AND ACCOUNTABLE NATURE, DANIEL FOUNDED DANIELSTEINS CONTRACTING, LLC IN JANUARY 2023 PROVIDING COMMERCIAL AND RESIDENTIAL EARTHWORK, GRADING, AND SITE WORK SERVICES THROUGHOUT THE ENTIRETY OF INDIANA.
